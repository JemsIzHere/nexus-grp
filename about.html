About Us

Nexus Group was founded back in 2003 with the belief of bridging the gap between human potential and technical excellence. It started as a training provider and academy known as Advanced Speech & Academic Professionals providing professional trainings and upskilling in Data Analytics and ICT. As the years go by, the company known as ASAP Nexus Academy has grown into a full-service technology company expanding beyond the classroom and into the heart of enterprise solutions.

Eventually ASAP Nexus ventured to Software Development and Data Analysis which birthed a new branch known as Nexus Core Systems, offering businesses not just the knowledge to grow, but the tools and systems to make it happen. What began as a passion for education evolved into a comprehensive suite of technology services, from building custom software solutions to delivering actionable insights through data. This natural progression reflected the company's core belief: The evolution of technology is inseparable from the evolution of human knowledge, organizations bold enough to embrace both will define what comes next.

Today, the two companies merged into the Nexus Group and now stands as a trusted partner for organizations looking to innovate, adapt, and lead in an ever-changing digital landscape.
